# Theme 1 — Slate + Emerald

**Mood**: Precise, modern, trustworthy. The Linear / Vercel register — generous whitespace, restrained colour, a single confident accent that does heavy semantic work.

**Who it's for**: Modern consultancies, dev shops, advisory firms that already self-identify as "tech-forward". The same buyer who would pick Linear over Jira, Notion over Confluence.

**The trade-off**: Emerald is doing double duty as both brand accent and the success / approval semantic in the app. That is a deliberate coherence win — the brand colour fires when a payment is received or an agent posts a journal — but it means we cannot later introduce a green semantic that is distinct from the brand. If the brand evolves we must pivot both at once.

**Why I lead with this**: The micro-mark (rotated square) is one SVG path, scales cleanly to a 16px favicon, and Inter Bold needs no custom hinting. It is the only direction that ships in a single design day. The other two are richer concepts but slower to land cleanly.
