# Theme 2 — Ink + Indigo

**Mood**: Quiet authority. The Stripe Atlas / Linear Pro register — deep ink background, paper-white type, a single indigo rule doing the visual work. Reads as "we are the people your board hired" rather than "we are the people your engineers love".

**Who it's for**: M&A advisory, management consultancies, accounting and law practices, finance-heavy buyers. Anyone whose buyer is a partner or CFO, not a developer. The explicit "for professional services" secondary line tells the brand story before any copy loads — useful in cold outreach where the logo appears on a deck cover slide before any pitch.

**The trade-off**: The stacked lockup loses its secondary line below ~32px, so favicon and mobile chrome fall back to a monogrammed "A" or the divider mark alone — neither is as distinctive as Theme 1's rotated square. Indigo is also a more crowded brand colour (Stripe, Vercel, Linear all flirt with it) so the differentiation has to come from the typographic treatment, not the hue.

**Why not lead**: Slower to land — the optical kerning on the secondary line and the divider proportions need a real typographer's eye. Higher risk of looking generic if executed at speed.
